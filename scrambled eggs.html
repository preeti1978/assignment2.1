Scrambled Eggs

Eggs are one of my favourite foods. Here is a recipe for a deliciously rich scrambled eggs.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbs butter
  • 2 tbs cream

Method

  1. Melt butter in a frying pan over medium heat.
  2. Gently mix the eggs and cream in a bowl
  3. Once butter has melted add cream and eggs
  4. Using a spatula fold the eggs from the edge of the pan to the centre every 20 seconds (as if you are making an omlette)
  5. When the eggs are still moist remove from heat (it will continueto cook on plate until served)
